About Krishi Kalyan Seva

It is a smart voice assistant that is able to understand the local language of the farmer and answer his/her queries in his/her local language itself. The voice assistant should be intelligent enough to support multiple Indian languages and make the conversation look more like human interaction.

Farmers will get answers for their queries related to type of crop that can be grown and the fertilizers to be used. It also tells the correct price for the crop so that the farmer is not duped by the agent. It is also capable to calculate which crop can be grown in what area so as to maximise the profit."

Krishi Kalyan Seva is able to predict the weather of the local region over the next week. It also tries to understand farmer's sentiments and provides helpline numbers if it realizes the farmer is in depression.
